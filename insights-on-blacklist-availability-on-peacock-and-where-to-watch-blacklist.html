    Why is Blacklist Not on Peacock?

    The absence of “The Blacklist” on Peacock is attributed to a contractual agreement between NBC and Netflix. As the show’s broadcaster, NBCUniversal has granted exclusive streaming rights to “The Blacklist” to Netflix in the United States and select other regions. Given that Peacock is a newer streaming service, it is currently in the process of securing rights to popular shows.

    While there is a chance that “The Blacklist” might join Peacock in the future, there is no definite confirmation at this time. For now, viewers can enjoy “The Blacklist” on Netflix, where it is readily accessible. Additionally, episodes or seasons of the series can be purchased on platforms like Amazon Prime Video, iTunes, and Google Play.

    When Will Blacklist Be on Peacock?

    Absolutely, The Blacklist Season 10 will eventually arrive on Peacock. The season’s culmination on NBC will take place with the final episode airing on July 13. Subsequently, the last two episodes of Blacklist Season 10 will be available on Peacock. The penultimate episode is set to premiere on July 14, followed by the anticipated season finale on July 21.

    During the climactic episode of this much-awaited season, a crucial decision concerning the fate of the FBI’s Reddington task force will be made. This pivotal moment is certain to keep fans eagerly awaiting the outcome of this riveting storyline. Peacock serves as a convenient platform for viewers to delve into The Blacklist, offering the flexibility to watch the show at their own pace.

    Whether you’ve been following the series since its inception or are just starting, Peacock provides a platform to catch up on the latest episodes and experience the captivating twists and turns.

    Where to Watch Blacklist?

    “The Blacklist” can be easily streamed via various platforms such as Amazon Prime Video, Sky Go, and Now TV. Furthermore, for those who prefer to own episodes, the option to purchase and download is available on platforms like Amazon Video, Sky Store, Apple TV, Google Play Movies, Microsoft Store, Chili, and Rakuten TV.

    With its widespread availability, this critically acclaimed series offers audiences numerous avenues to immerse themselves in its captivating storyline and intriguing characters at their convenience.

    When will The Blacklist Season 10 Air New Episodes?

    “The Blacklist” Season 10 will air new episodes to conclude its run on Thursdays. The decision to shift the show back to Thursdays may be influenced by the summer months when viewers may prefer spending Sundays with family rather than watching TV. By airing midweek, the show can still attract a significant audience.

    The season commenced on June 1 with a double-bill of episodes. Following this premiere, the schedule for the remaining episodes is as follows: June 8 (New episode), June 15 (No new episode), June 22 (New episode), June 29 (New episode), July 6 (New episode), and the series finale on July 13. With six episodes left in the series, the storyline is currently centered on Congressman Hudson, determined to dismantle the Task Force.

    His hidden motives are evident, and viewers are eager to uncover them as the narrative unfolds. “The Blacklist” devotees can anticipate thrilling episodes on Thursdays, ensuring their engagement until the series wraps up with its highly-anticipated finale.

    The Blacklist

    The Blacklist is a renowned American crime thriller television series recognized for its compelling storyline and stellar cast. Devised by Jon Bokenkamp and crafted by John Eisendrath, the show stars James Spader as Raymond Reddington, a notorious fugitive collaborating with the FBI to track down dangerous criminals.

    The series also showcases talented actors Megan Boone, Diego Klattenhoff, Ryan Eggold, Amir Arison, Hisham Tawfiq, and Harry Lennix. Produced by Sony Pictures Television, Universal Television, and Davis Entertainment, “The Blacklist” features a strong line-up of executive producers including John Eisendrath, John Davis, and John Fox, who have been involved throughout the series.

    Jon Bokenkamp, the show’s creator, also served as an executive producer for the initial eight seasons. Additionally, director Joe Carnahan and lead actor James Spader held executive producer roles. The series premiered on NBC on September 23, 2013, and concluded on July 13, 2023.

    From its inception, the show made a significant impact, with the pilot episode attracting 12.6 million viewers in the United States. Throughout its tenure, “The Blacklist” garnered critical acclaim, including a Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Award and multiple Emmy nominations. It received nominations for Golden Globe Awards, Saturn Awards, and People’s Choice Awards, cementing its status as a beloved production.

    Extending beyond television success, “The Blacklist” has expanded into a vibrant tie-in media franchise, encompassing comics, novels, and a video game. Its popularity led to a spin-off titled “The Blacklist: Redemption,” which aired on NBC from February to April 2017. Helmed by Jon Bokenkamp and John Eisendrath, the spin-off shared the same universe as the original series, focusing on Tom Keen, portrayed by Ryan Eggold.

    “The Blacklist” has indelibly left its mark on the crime thriller genre, captivating audiences with its intricate plots, multifaceted characters, and stellar performances, solidifying its place in television entertainment.
