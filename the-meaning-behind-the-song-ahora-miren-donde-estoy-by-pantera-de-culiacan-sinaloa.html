    These lines pay homage to the singer’s family and heritage. They emphasize the importance of family and never forgetting their roots. Pantera de Culiacan Sinaloa proudly proclaims their Mexican identity, inherited from their mother. They also mention their father, who has passed away but remains in their heart. The lyrics convey the lesson of perseverance learned from their father, serving as a constant reminder to never give up.
