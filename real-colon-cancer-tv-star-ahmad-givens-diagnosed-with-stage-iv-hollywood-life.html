    Real’s History On Reality TV

    If you’re not familiar with “Real,” allow us to bring you up to speed. After competing to win the heart of Tiffany “New York” Pollard Vh1’s “I Love New York,” he was offered his own spin-off series with fellow contestant — and brother — Kamal “Chance” Givens.
