    CULVER CITY, CALIFORNIA: As Pat Sajak bids farewell to 'Wheel of Fortune,' his legacy as a beloved host remains intact. Since taking the helm in 1981, Sajak has elevated the show to new heights, becoming the face of the popular show. While his co-host, Vanna White, will continue her role on the board, fans wonder who will be the next host.

    Among the contenders, Sajak's daughter, Maggie, a social correspondent for 'Wheel of Fortune', has emerged as a potential successor. Having grown up in the world of the game show, she possesses a deep understanding of the job. However, some argue that Vanna White, who briefly stepped in for Sajak in 2019, should rightfully assume the position. Although there has been no official statement from the executives, Maggie and White could potentially become the new duo leading 'Wheel of Fortune'.

    Is Pat Sajak bald?

    Amid discussions about Sajak's retirement, a question that sticks out is: Is Pat Sajak bald? A memorable moment from the show provides the answer. Contrary to popular belief, Sajak is not bald. However, his hair has been a subject of intrigue for devoted 'Wheel of Fortune' fans. While he has consistently denied wearing a toupee, a surprising incident from an episode aired in April 2008 shed light on the truth.

    Also Read: 'Days of our Lives' star Kyle Brandt surprises Vanna White as he admits she was his childhood crush

    The episode started off as usual, with Sajak and White engaging in playful banter before bidding farewell to the audience. However, this particular episode became unforgettable when Sajak used the moment to reveal something unexpected to his co-star. Acknowledging audience's questions about his hair, he took off his hairpiece on live TV! White appeared genuinely surprised as she witnessed his reveal and was left momentarily speechless.

    Also Read: 'Jeopardy!' host Ken Jennings hails 'Wheel of Fortune' host Pat Sajak as 'more fun' than his own forerunner Alex Trebek

    'Happy April Fool's Day!'

    Sajak seemed relieved to finally be "honest" with everyone, expressing his newfound freedom during the episode. He bid farewell to the crowd, exclaiming, "Happy April 1st, everybody," while White continued to react in disbelief. The episode generated widespread speculation, with many recognizing it as an April Fool's Day prank intended to address the rumors surrounding Sajak's hair.

    However, fans appreciated the humor behind the stunt, praising the makeup and hair departments for their impeccable execution. The consensus among viewers was that his bald look was, in fact, a wig, further dispelling the notion that he was genuinely bald. In reality, Sajak hair is his own. The April 1 reveal was simply a lighthearted joke, though rumors and speculation persist to this day. The clip of that memorable moment continues to captivate fans. Interestingly, his hair has garnered a similar level of fan fascination as Alex Trebek's mustache, becoming a notable element in the overall appeal of the show.
