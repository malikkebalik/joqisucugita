    Moga Mogami is a Japanese idol, actor, model, and performer who is a former member of the female idol group Dempagumi.inc, formed in 2008 by producer Maiko Fukushima. Moreover, Moga Mogami has been a part of several shows and movies such as Kai-soku Shōjo, Ultraman Ginga S, Ultraman New Generation Chronicle, Jūhan Shuttai!, Magic x Warrior Magi Majo Pures!, Apoyan: Hashiru Kokusai Kūkō, Ultraman Ginga S The Movie, Yamikin Ushijima-kun The Final, Kurogarasu Part 1, Kurogarasu Part 2, and Yamikin Ushijima-kun Part3.
