    Who is Kyra Zagorsky?

    Kyra Zagorsky is an American actress who is best known for her role in the TV series ‘Helix’.

    Furthermore, she has also appeared in popular television series such as ‘Supernatural’, ‘Polaris’, and ‘The 100’.

    Kyra Zagorsky: Birth Facts, Early Life

    Kyra Zagorsky was born on June 6, 1976, in New York, USA. That makes her 46 years of age.

    She was born to a Polish-Lithuanian father and a European Jewish mother. However, she has not revealed the name of her parents. She has also not talked about her siblings if any.

    Kyra grew up playing sports and dancing in the mountains of a small town in Colorado. She trained in kickboxing, capoeira, and Filipino stick and sword fighting during her early days.

    Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is mixed (Polish-Lithuanian and European Jewish).

    Talking about her education, Kyra Zagorsky was enrolled at Southern Oregon University in Ashland where she received a BFA in theatre. In 2006, she completed her Master of Fine Arts degree in acting from the University of California, Irvine.

    Kyra Zagorsky got her first role in a 2004 film Power Corps with the role of Trish followed by her role as Amy in Green Season. After that, she was seen in the TV series Stargate Atlantis.

    Some of her appeared movies include The ABCs of Death, Toxin, Vendetta, and S.W.A.T.: Under Siege. Furthermore, she has done various TV series such as Continuum, Stargate: Atlantis, Stargate Universe SGU, Supernatural, Fringe, Falling Skies, and Divine.
