    Bella Hadid, the superstar model who’s been making waves on and off the runway, is back with a bang! Bella’s returned in a whirlwind of glamor, gracing the cover of Perfect magazine’s spring/summer 2024 edition. But here’s the kicker – she’s pulling a Debbie Harry, channeling that ’78 vibe by wearing… well, nothing but a cheeky poster!

    Bella Hadid, the runway sensation, is back to take the fashion world by storm after her break, battling Lyme disease. And guess what? She’s not just back; she’s bringing sass, style, and a dash of cheekiness!

    The 27-year-old is gracing the cover of Perfect’s Issue Six for Spring 2024, following a year-long break from modeling.

    Additionally, based on an Instagram post, Hadid appeared to be heavily involved in the project: she art directed the entire shoot that took place at the end of 2023 and “handpicked” the creative team, which included photographer Bryce Anderson and artist Corbin Shaw.

    Sporting nothing but a poster and a bold attitude on the cover of Perfect magazine’s spring/summer 2024 issue, Bella’s got tongues wagging and jaws dropping.

    Seated on a chair, Bella exudes poise and confidence. The only thing covering her is a statement poster that says, “Absolutely nothing.” Nothing at all.” Though, as you can see, she does not always take things literally.

    She is wearing stylish black briefs underneath that eye-catching white message, adding a 2024 twist. And those amazing gold hoops earrings—did we mention them? It’s sass meets beauty!

    This isn’t just a one-trick cover wonder either. Nope, Bella’s serving looks in four different cover shots.

    There’s a touch of ‘Austin Powers’ retro coolness, a groovy ’60s-inspired ensemble that screams flower power, and an edgy pairing of black lace panties with a T-shirt reading “Screaming in Perfect English.” Bella’s fashion game? Clearly on point.

    View this post on Instagram

    Despite taking a breather from the catwalk, Bella’s been keeping busy. From teasing fans with behind-the-scenes TikToks to appearing in a Marc Jacobs shoot that was out-of-this-world (literally, it was extraterrestrial-themed), she’s been slaying the style game.

    While Bella’s been living life low-key, hanging out at family gatherings and stepping out with a new love interest, Adan Banuelos, this magazine cover signals her triumphant return. And boy, are we ready for it! Bella Hadid’s back in town, folks, and 2024 might just be her year to own the runway once again.
