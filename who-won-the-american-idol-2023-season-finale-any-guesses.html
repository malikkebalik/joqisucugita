    Hello, fans of the popular show American Idol. As far as we know, season 21 of American Idol is currently being shown on TV. This show is known all over the world. This season ends because the big finale is Sunday, May 21. People had been waiting for this moment for a long time.

    Fans are very excited to see who wins season 21 of American Idol. This show has been on for a long time. It has been popular for 20 seasons. The winner of the 21st season is about to be announced. If you read this post to the conclusion, you’ll find that we’ve provided every last detail of the performance and Twitter reactions.

    The Wait is Over! Winner of American Idol’s 2023 Season Final Is..

    The winner of American Idol’s 2023 season Finale is William “Iam” Tongi.

    ✨🥇✨@wtongi is your next #IDOL! pic.twitter.com/Mpj7EFTeHA

    — American Idol (@AmericanIdol) May 22, 2023

    On Sunday night, host Ryan Seacrest said that Iam got the most votes from fans, putting him ahead of Megan Danielle and Colin Stough, who were also in the top three. Before he was named the winner of American Idol season 6 (the 21st season overall), fans of the ABC singing competition show saw how he improved as an artist every week on stage to win the ultimate title.

    American Idol judges Lionel Richie, Katy Perry, and Luke Bryan were moved by Iam’s emotional rendition of James Blunt’s “Monsters. After he won a trip to Hollywood, the Hawaiian man kept the judges and people watching from home impressed with his song choices and personal acts.

    Unsurprisingly, this was also the case during the finale after belting out memorable versions of “Making Memories of Us” by Keith Urban and “Cool Down” by Kolohe Kai. To bring it full circle, Iam gave it his all when he performed his new single “I’ll Be Seeing You” for his last song.
