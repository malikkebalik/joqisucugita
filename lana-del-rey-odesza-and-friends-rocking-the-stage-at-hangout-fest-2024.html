    Lana Del Rey, Odesza, and Friends Rocking the Stage at Hangout Fest 2024

    Hang onto your festival hats because Alabamas Hangout Music Festival is about to drop a lineup hotter than a summer bonfire! Lana Del Reys ethereal vibes, Zach Bryans soul-stirring tunes, and Odeszas electrifying beats are just the tip of this musical iceberg.

    24 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Hang onto your festival hats because Alabama’s Hangout Music Festival is about to drop a lineup hotter than a summer bonfire! Lana Del Rey’s ethereal vibes, Zach Bryan’s soul-stirring tunes, and Odesza’s electrifying beats are just the tip of this musical iceberg.

    Prepare for a symphony of sensations as The Chainsmokers, Dominic Fike, Cage the Elephant, and the iconic Nelly hit the stage, turning it into a sonic wonderland. And that’s not all – Chappell Roan will mesmerize, while NLE Choppa, Doechii, and the double dose of Nelly bring the hip-hop heat like never before!

    Get ready for the infectious energy of All Time Low, the rock-your-world talent of A Day to Remember, and Koe Wetzel’s electrifying presence. With Flyana Boss, David Kushner, and Megan Moroney in the mix, this festival is about to set a new standard for musical escapades!

    But wait, it gets better! Subtronics and the triumphant return of Alison Wonderland will have you grooving into the night. Don’t miss Alabama’s own Jessie Murph lighting up the stage alongside the spellbinding Alison Wonderland.

    The festival boasts a local spotlight with Qveen Herby, Chromeo, Paul Russell, and the Beaches ready to dazzle. Let Del Water Gap, Eliza Rose, and Alana Springsteen add their musical brushstrokes to this vibrant canvas.

    Missed the “advance sale”? There’s hope yet! Register for early access before tickets go live on Friday at 12 pm ET.

    Now, about those tickets – let’s talk tiers! Ranging from $299 for 3-day GA access to the VIP delights at $1,299 featuring cocktails and side-stage pools. And for the cream of the crop, the Super VIP package at $2,299 offers an exclusive festival kingdom.

    So, set your reminders, save the dates, and prepare for a musical expedition like no other at Alabama’s Hangout Fest 2024. It’s not just a festival; it’s a harmonious voyage you won’t want to miss!
