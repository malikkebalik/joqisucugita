    Samuel Holloway Car Accident: 64-year-old Northeast, D.C. man arrested in hit-and-run of family caught on camera; Samuel Holloway arrest & charges.

    In a recent development, authorities have apprehended a suspect in connection with a hit-and-run incident that occurred in early October of this year. The incident, which was captured on camera, has raised concerns among the public.

    In a recent development, 64-year-old Samuel Holloway, a resident of Northeast, D.C., was arrested and charged on Wednesday for his involvement in a hit-and-run incident that occurred on October 4. The victims of this unfortunate incident were a mother, her son, and their pet dog.

    In a shocking incident, a mother, her ten-year-old daughter, six-year-old son, and their 15-month-old family dog were caught on camera being struck by a dark-colored Nissan Armada. The SUV reportedly accelerated into an intersection where the Northeast D.C. family was already in the crosswalk. In a distressing incident, a six-year-old boy was seemingly pinned by the SUV tyre.

    According to police, the boy was transported to a nearby hospital with injuries that are not considered life-threatening. Additionally, the mother and the dog are reported to be in good condition.
