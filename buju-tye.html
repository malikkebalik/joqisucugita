    Buju TYE- Biography

    Buju TYE (BujuToYourEars) is a Nigerian singer, rapper, record producer, and composer best known for his songs Catch a Vibe, A Day in Lagos, Aje, L’Enu, Spiritual, L’Enu remix ft Burna Boy, and Outside (Recent release on 19th May 2021). (Recently released on May 19, 2021). Burna Boy signed him to Spaceship Records, and a remix of his hit L’enu starring Burna Boy was used as an opening hymn for him. After Burna Boy and his sister Nissi Ogulu, he is the third musician signed to Spaceship Collective.

    His contract with the record label was claimed to have expired in April, and he refused to extend it, preferring to concentrate on his profession and himself. He was named Revelation of the Year at the City People Music Awards. Buju acquired his moniker from a Jamaican reggae artist named Buju Banton. BujuToyourEars is his stage moniker.

    Buju TYE- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

    Buju TYE was born on May 14, 1998, making him 23 years old as of 2022. His birthplace is Lagos, Nigeria. Daniel Benson is his true name. He is of Nigerian origin and of Nigerian-Black ethnicity. His birthplace is Akwa Ibom State. He spent his early youth in Gbagada before migrating to Ogun State with his family. He has had a passion for music since he was a child. His mother died as a result of cancer. His horoscope sign is Taurus, and he practices Christianity. He has not revealed anything about his parents’ names or the identities of his siblings to the public.

    Buju began writing music when he was in junior secondary school, according to his educational history. During this period, he was known as Drizzle, but he eventually changed his stage name to ‘Buju.’ He began rapping and eventually joined the choir. Then he began performing solo at valedictory services and continued his interest at university. He spent time honing his technique and being inspired by artists such as Burna Boy, Buju Banton, Damian Marley, and J Hus. Buju completed and graduated from Akwa Ibom State University. He had an industrial training job while in university that he disliked. Buju was then suspended because he was so focused on his music that he would sometimes arrive late at his venue and use his spare time to do additional music. He got arrested while listening to one of his Buju Banton songs.
