    Rapper LL Cool J's wife of 25 years looks stunning in a recent post shared on her Instagram page as she promotes her jewelry line.

    Rapper LL Cool J's wife, Simone Smith recently gave her followers a taste of her youthful beauty in a recent post on her Instagram page. The rapper's wife wore a white and yellow coordinated outfit and penned a caption that read:

    "Happy Labor Day!! @simoneismithjewelry and @sisterlovemjb is my Labor Of Love?."

    Simone Smith and LL Cool J at the Vanity Fair Oscar Party on February 26, 2017. | Photo: Getty Images

    Simone was dressed in a basic white T-shirt and a bright blue pair of jeans. Over her white tee, she layered a yellow and gray striped button-down shirt that was tied in a knot at her waist.

    Besides her radiant face and smile, the stars of the show were her long golden braids and her stunning bamboo hoop earrings made by her jewelry company.

    The breathtaking beauty had her nails done in a vibrant mix of colors which only added to the eye-catching colorful effect of her post. Adoring comments quickly lined up beneath Simone's post, gushing over how beautiful she looked and complimenting her company's design.

    Simone Smith Jewelry was officially launched in 2011 after the strong and incredible Simone partnered up with popular singer, Mary J Blige, to make it a success.

    Although many of Simone's followers are very supportive of her jewelry company, not many of them are aware of the touching story behind its origin. It all began in 2004 when Simone was diagnosed with a rare form of bone cancer, chondrosarcoma.

    Together with her family, the valiant entrepreneur fought the disease and conquered it. It was during this challenging time that she realized that starting her business was the best way to donate to fighting cancer and helping those who needed help. She said to FOX business:

    "Every season is a season for giving."

    The rapper's wife decided to put all her energy into her jewelry business. A portion of the proceeds she made would go directly to the American Cancer Society and into the fight against cancer through her "Beat Cancer Like a Boss" campaign.

    Simone Smith Jewelry was officially launched in 2011 after the strong and incredible Simone partnered up with popular singer, Mary J Blige, to make it a success.

    All along on her crusade against cancer, Simone's doting husband and their children were there with her every step of the way. The jewelry line-owner tied the knot with the rapper almost three decades ago in August 1995.

    They welcomed their first child, their son, Najee, six years before they tied the knot, and their second child, their daughter, Italia, was born four years before their nuptials.

    Their last two kids, their girls, Samaria and Nina, were born after they tied the knot. Twenty-five years later, after many challenges including a true boss against cancer, this stunning mom of four is still standing strong and proud as ever. Only a superstar could do that.
