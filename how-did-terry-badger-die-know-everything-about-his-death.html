    Who was Terry Badger? Know more about his life

    Terry Badger, also known as Terry Badger III, was a 13-year-old boy from Covington, Indiana, who was a very talented baseball player. He was born on August 9, 2009, in Danville, Illinois, in the United States.

    Terry was a sports freak who loved to participate in sports, especially baseball and basketball. He played baseball for the Fury, Nitro, and Braves and also represented Covington throughout the seasons. He played for the local Covington teams as well.

    Terry Badger III was the son of Terry II and Robyn Edwards Badger, and the name of his sister is Zoe Badger. His family members also included his grandparents, Terry & Mary Badger, Sr., Ron & Sherry Edwards, and Barbara Badger.

    How did Terry Badger die? His funeral details

    The Covington-based baseball player, Terry Badger, passed away at the age of 13 on Monday, March 6, 2023, at his home. According to reports, Terry unexpectedly took his own life as a result of bullying, and thus the cause of his death is suicide. His unexpected death has come as a real shock for all his loved ones and has left them in deep pain.

    A visitation will be organised for the Terry Badger family and friends from 12 to 4 p.m. on Sunday, March 12, 2023, at Covington Middle School Gym, 5th Street in Covington, IN. The 13-year-old will be laid to rest in Mount Hope Cemetery in Covington, Indiana.
